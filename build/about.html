Welcome

Cuplux Coffee is a family owned, drive-up only coffee shop serving specialty level coffee beverages and local pastries. First time patrons should expect the same quality coffee that they get at their favorite sit-down shop, in a convenient drive-thru style manner.

We proudly brew Stumptown Coffee and feature a rotating selection of guest roasters from the Carolinas. Our craft beverage menu includes a select variety of coffee, tea and espresso-based beverages all made from the best ingredients possible. Food wise, we offer a light spattering of local pastries, house made granola and parfaits.

If you have any questions or are new to coffee, please let us know. Our knowledgeable and courteous baristas will help you along your way!
